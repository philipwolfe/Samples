Welcome to the Common Tasks QuickStart

The Common Task QuickStart is designed to help you quickly find the answers to your most frequent programming questions. Unlike other QuickStarts that are meant to be read from start to end, this section may be used as a topical reference. If there are topics not addressed here that you'd like to see in future releases, please drop us an email at qswish@microsoft.com.
