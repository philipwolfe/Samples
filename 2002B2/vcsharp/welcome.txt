Welcome to the Microsoft Visual C#.NET samples!

In the CSharpSamples directory you will find a variety of sample projects that demonstrate various programming tasks.  Abstracts for each of the samples are located in the Visual C# documentation, accessible through the developer environment or the external MSDN viewer.  The sample abstracts include links for opening the sample in the development environment and for copying the sample's source files to your hard drive.
