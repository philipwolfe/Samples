Step 2 of 5: Add Labels & Dropdown

  1. Now, let's begin placing controls on the form starting with three descriptive labels. Drag three Label controls from the Toolbox onto the form surface.

  2. Update the text of the labels by clicking on the labels one at a time and changing the Text property in the Properties window. From left to right, update the text of the labels to: Choose Customer, Choose Start Date, and Choose End Date.

  3. We want to align the labels vertically, so select all of the labels by holding down the Ctrl key and clicking each control.

  4. From the Format menu, choose Align - Tops. The top edge of the labels will now be aligned vertically.

  5. Now, drag a DropdownList control from the Toolbox onto the form beneath the Choose Customer label. In the Properties window, change the ID property to ddlCustomers
